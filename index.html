About PDX Neighbors

Portland's really easy to navigate because, for the most part, it's laid out on a grid. The Willamette River divides the east and west sides of town, and Burnside Street splits it north and south. The result is that most of Portland lies within five quadrants: North, Northwest, Northeast, Southwest and Southeast.
